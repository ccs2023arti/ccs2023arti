We change the motion duration from 8 s to 16 s, with increments of 2 s. For each duration, we perform 10 attempts of WeakCamID.

8 10 12 14 16 (sss)

Different durations of motion occurring within the motion detection area of the camera (with a subscription or in live view mode) may generate varying wireless traffic volumes.

Total attempts = 11×4×5×10= 2, 200 attempts.

Calculation method:

For localization error:
Success rate = Successful cases / Total cases
For localization time:
F1 Score = (Precision * Recall) / (Precision + Recall)
